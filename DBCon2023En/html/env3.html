To minimize environmental impacts in case of emergencies and flexibly respond to changing environmental regulations, Dongbu Corporation is providing appropriate environmental education to employees. Environment protection campaign

All employees of Dongbu Corporation are performing green management to save environments and take the lead in practicing by participating in campaign activities such as <Zero Disposables> and <Plogging Dongbu>.

Development of eco-friendly technologies

Dongbu Corporation is practicing development of eco-friendly technologies to achieve a company vision of sustainable green construction and build the company’s sustainable management status through carbon neutrality. In 2022, we obtained green technology certification of the construction waste reduction technology from KIAT (Korea Institute for Advancement of Technology). We will continue to strengthen the company’s competitiveness by developing eco-friendly technologies, and also take the lead in protecting environments.
