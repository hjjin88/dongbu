Global
initiative

For sustainable green construction, Dongbu Corporation has been working to address problems and issues related to climate crisis and environmental contamination that the global society is facing, such as global warming, carbon neutrality, waste generation, etc. Major key principles of SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals) adopted by the UN were reflected in the management activities of our company, and we will strive to be a company that takes the lead in and practicing of ESG management by participating actively in global initiatives regarding the environment, society, and governance in the coming years.
