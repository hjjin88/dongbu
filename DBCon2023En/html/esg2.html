ESG Management

Outline of ESG Management Importance Assessment Organization Chart and Main Task
Process of importance assessment

To proceed with ESG management activities suitable for the circumstances of Dongbu Corporation, our own ESG issue pool was formed. The importance assessment was performed with regard to stakeholders and priorities were set in consideration of attention to social concerns and business effects. Based on importance assessment results, we unearthed key tasks for the ESG management of our company and analyzed risks and opportunity factors of Dongbu Corporation related to these.
