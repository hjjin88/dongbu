Status of shareholders’ meeting

In accordance with Article 15 (Convocation of Meeting) of articles of association of our company, Dongbu Corporation convenes a general shareholders’ meeting within 3 months from the end of the business year. Venues and agendas are being posted through DART in accordance with Article 542-4 of Commercial Act.

Guarantee of shareholders’ rights

Dongbu Coporation guarantees rights so that general shareholders can exercise shareholders’ rights in shareholders’ meetings.  All the shareholders are guaranteed to have 1 vote a week in accordance with Article 20 (shareholder’s vote) of articles of association of our company.

Announcement for shareholders’ proposal rights

Dongbu Corporation is creating an environment facilitating shareholders’ exercise of voting rights in accordance with relevant acts and allowing shareholders to propose agendas for shareholders’ meetings to the Board.

Minority shareholders’ rights
  • - In accordance with Article 542-6 (Minority Shareholders’ Rights) of Commercial Acts,
     we always listen to and collect the opinions of minority shareholders and guarantee
     the following rights related to shareholders’ meetings.
Rights to convene shareholders’ meeting
  • - In accordance with Article 542-6 of Commercial Acts, shareholders who have continued to
     hold stocks not less than 15/1,000 of the total number of stocks issued and outstanding for
     more than six months except for nonvoting stocks can request   an extraordinary general
     meeting be convened.
Shareholders’ rights to make proposals
  • - Based on relevant acts, shareholders having over a certain percentage of equity interest
     can propose to have certain items placed on the agendas of a shareholders’ meetings, in
     writing or electronic documents 6 weeks before the date of a shareholders’ meetings.
     In the case that such proposals exist, the company shall tabulate these agenda items for
     shareholders’ meetings, in accordance with internal procedures except for proposals of
     shareholders that violate laws , articles of association, or relevant acts.
