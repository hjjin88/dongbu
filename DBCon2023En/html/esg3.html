ESG Management Organization Chart and Main Task Dongbu Corporation set up the ESG committee within the Board in 2022 and incorporated ESG management CFT as an organization directly under the management team. As a multi-functional team, ESG management transcends barriers between departments and has been operated to achieve objectives of the ESG management which is stable and sustainable in many ways.
